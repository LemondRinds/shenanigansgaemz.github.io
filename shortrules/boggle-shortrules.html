Boggle short rules

To play

  1. Shake the grid of cubes and place it on the table.
  2. Remove the lid and push unslotted cubes into holes.
  3. For 4 minutes: each player makes a list of 4+ letter words spelled from continuous strings of cubes on the grid, using cubes once max per word.
  4. Players recite their lists in turn. Cross out all words on more than one list; cross out non-words.
  5. Remaining words are worth their length minus 3, except affixes which are worth 1.
  6. Repeat until satisfied.

Affixes

Words made by attaching common pre- or suffixes to a base word that don't form a word with a distinct meaning are affixes.

  • "Findings" is find+ing+s, and so is an affix. It's worth 1 point.
  • "Mister" is mist+er, but "mister" as a term is distinct from "mist", and so is worth the full 3 points.

Common prefixes: de, dis, mis, out, over, pre, re, sub, un, under

Common postfixes: s, es, ed, ied, er, ier, ing, ly, y
